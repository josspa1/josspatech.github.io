Step 1

Download from your bank

Log into your bank's website, find the download or export option, and save a CSV file. Most banks have this under "Statements" or "Activity."

Step 2

Import into PocketBudJet

Tap Import, pick your file. The Smart CSV Mapper auto-detects columns — date, amount, description. Works with any bank format.

Step 3

Watch it come alive

Transactions fill in, categories auto-sort, budget bars populate, and your spending trends become visible for the first time.

Done
