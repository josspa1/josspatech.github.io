The Complete PocketBudJet Guide

Every screen. Every feature. Every setting. A visual walkthrough of everything PocketBudJet can do for your finances.

Watch the Full Walkthrough

Follow along as we tour every major app feature in just 8 slides

Slide 1 of 8

Your financial command center

The dashboard shows your net worth, recent transactions, budget progress, and upcoming bills — all on one screen. Everything updates in real time.

Slide 2 of 8

Every dollar, tracked and categorized

View, search, filter, and edit all your transactions. Auto-categorization learns your patterns. Split transactions across categories.

Slide 3 of 8

Five ways to get data in

Snap receipts with your camera, use a WiFi document scanner, import CSV or PDF bank statements, or enter manually. No bank login ever required.

Slide 4 of 8

Set limits. Track progress. Stay on target.

Choose a template or build your own. Category limits with color-coded progress bars warn you before you overspend, not after.

Slide 5 of 8

Never miss a due date

See every upcoming bill on a visual calendar with green payday dots. Know exactly when money comes in and when it goes out.

Slide 6 of 8

See your debt-free date

Snowball or avalanche — pick your strategy. PocketBudJet calculates your freedom date and shows how much interest you'll save.

Slide 7 of 8

Your personal financial advisor

Your AI coach spots trends, warns about overspending, celebrates wins, and answers questions about your money. Like having an advisor in your pocket.

Slide 8 of 8

Your data stays on your device

All data encrypted on your phone. Optional WiFi household sync. No cloud, no data selling, no bank credentials. Export in six formats anytime.
