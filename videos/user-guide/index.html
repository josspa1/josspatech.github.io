The Complete PocketBudJet Guide

Every screen. Every feature. Every setting. A visual walkthrough of everything PocketBudJet can do for your finances.

Watch the Full Walkthrough

Follow along as we tour every feature across 27 chapters

Slide 1 of 27

Welcome & Onboarding

Download PocketBudJet, run the setup wizard, create your first account, and start your 35-day free trial. The wizard walks you through your name, currency, accounts, income (gross pay and take-home), bills, goals, and budgeting style — everything the app needs to get smart from day one.

Slide 2 of 27

Your Dashboard

Income, spending, and net cards at the top. Spending pace indicator shows if you're on track. Widgets for quick actions. Anomaly alerts flag unusual transactions. The spending pace indicator compares where you are vs. where you should be this far into the month.

Slide 3 of 27

Adding Transactions

Tap +, enter amount, pick category, done. Split transactions across categories. Add attachments like receipt photos. Use presets for recurring entries. One tip: spell merchant names the same way every time — 'Daily Grind' not 'DAILY GRIND' or 'Daily Grind Coffee' — so they group correctly in reports.

Slide 4 of 27

Importing Data

Import CSV, OFX, QFX, QIF, XLSX, or PDF bank statements. Smart Mapper auto-detects columns. Duplicate detection prevents double-counting. Import at least three months of history — more data means smarter AI and better reports. For step-by-step instructions on downloading and importing bank files, see our Import Bank Data video.

Slide 5 of 27

Scanning Documents

Scan receipts with your camera. OCR reads merchant, amount, and date. WiFi scanner support connects to your home ADF scanner for batch processing. Even reads handwriting. As of launch, PocketBudJet is the only budgeting app we know of with WiFi scanner support. Feed a stack of receipts into your scanner's tray and walk away.

Slide 6 of 27

Managing Budgets

Five budget templates or build your own. Category limits with color-coded progress bars. Rollover unused budget. Health overlay shows your overall position. Variance reports compare planned vs. actual.

Slide 7 of 27

Bills & Recurring Transactions

Visual bill calendar with due dates. Green dots mark paydays. Set up recurring income and expenses. Smart prediction estimates upcoming bills based on patterns.

Slide 8 of 27

Savings & Goals

Set savings goals with target dates. Create sinking funds for planned expenses. Purchase wishlist with a 30-day cool-off period to curb impulse spending. What-if calculator shows trade-offs. The 30-day cool-off is a nudge — add something you want to buy, and PBJ waits 30 days before counting it. You'd be surprised how many 'must-haves' don't survive the wait.

Slide 9 of 27

Crushing Debt

Track all debts in one place. Choose avalanche (highest interest first) or snowball (smallest balance first). See your debt-free date. What-if scenarios show how extra payments accelerate payoff. Knowing your exact debt-free date changes everything. It turns an overwhelming pile of debt into a countdown.

Slide 10 of 27

Investments & Net Worth

Track stocks, crypto (19 coins supported), and real estate values via Zillow estimates. Monitor your credit score. See insurance policies alongside assets for a complete net worth picture.

Slide 11 of 27

Reports & Analytics

Over 10 report types: spending by category, income vs. expense, year-over-year comparisons, anomaly dashboard, tax summary, and custom reports you design yourself.

Slide 12 of 27

AI Financial Coach

Personalized insights based on your spending patterns. Opt-in only. Spots trends you'd miss, warns about unusual activity, and sends a weekly financial recap. The more data you give it, the smarter it gets. Import a few months of history and the AI has real patterns to work with.

Slide 13 of 27

AI Assistant

Ask questions about your money in plain English. 'How much did I spend on dining last month?' or 'Am I over budget?' Eleven query types, all processed on your device.

Slide 14 of 27

Find Any Feature Instantly

PocketBudJet has dozens of features — you won't stumble across all of them. Search for transactions by amount or merchant. Search for features: try 'mileage' for tax tracking, 'voice' for voice shortcuts, 'retirement' for the planner, or 'colorblind' for accessibility options. If you're ever wondering whether PBJ can do something, just search for it. This is how you'll find features like mileage tracking, transaction rules, the retirement planner, and accessibility options that you might not find just by tapping around.

Slide 15 of 27

Export & Sharing

Export in six formats: CSV, XLSX, OFX, PDF, JSON, and encrypted backup. Generate an accountant-ready tax report. Run a LAN server to transfer files to your computer.

Slide 16 of 27

PC Web Dashboard

Access PocketBudJet from any browser on your local network. Scan the QR code to pair. Five tabs: Dashboard, Transactions, Budgets, Reports, Settings. Full create/edit/delete. Real-time sync with your phone. Great for importing bank files you downloaded on your computer — drag and drop right into the browser.

Slide 17 of 27

Tax Features

Tag transactions with 8 IRS categories. Generate a tax summary for your accountant. Quarterly estimated tax calculator. Built-in mileage tracker for business deductions.

Slide 18 of 27

Household Sync

Sync with family members over WiFi or Bluetooth — no internet needed. Pair with a QR code. Joint budgets with individual spending visibility. Automatic conflict resolution for simultaneous edits.

Slide 19 of 27

Calendar View

Monthly calendar showing transaction dots on each day. Bill due dates and payday markers. Color-coded by category. Tap any day to see that day's transactions.

Slide 20 of 27

Bookmarks & Rules

Bookmark important transactions for quick access. Create auto-categorization rules by merchant name. Bulk edit multiple transactions at once. Manage category labels. Rules are a time-saver. Set one for 'Daily Grind → Coffee' and every future Daily Grind transaction categorizes itself.

Slide 21 of 27

Accessibility

Dark mode, high contrast mode, and colorblind-friendly palettes. App lock with biometrics. Large text support. Full screen reader compatibility.

Slide 22 of 27

Mindful Features

Mindful Budget View strips away numbers and shows simple progress rings. Time Cost Mode converts prices to hours of work. PBJ Stars gamify saving. Wellness check-ins track financial stress. Time Cost Mode is eye-opening. It converts every purchase into hours of your life. A $50 dinner becomes '2.5 hours of work.' It changes how you think about spending.

Slide 23 of 27

Retirement Planning

Track progress against Fidelity savings milestones. FIRE calculator for early retirement. Safe withdrawal rate projections. Retirement income estimator based on current savings rate.

Slide 24 of 27

Voice Shortcuts

Works with Siri and Google Assistant. Six built-in shortcuts: add transaction, check balance, budget status, and more. Create custom voice shortcuts for your most common actions.

Slide 25 of 27

Pricing

21-day free trial with full access. Then choose monthly, annual, or 3-year plans. Cancel anytime. No ads, no data selling — your subscription is our only revenue.

Slide 26 of 27

Privacy & Security

All data encrypted on your device. No bank login credentials ever stored. No cloud required. App lock with PIN or biometrics. Your financial data never leaves your phone unless you export it.

Slide 27 of 27

Need Help?

Email support@josspatech.com — we typically respond within 24 hours. Use the in-app feedback form for bug reports and feature requests. Visit josspatech.com/how-to for video guides on importing data, setting budgets, debt payoff, receipt scanning, and more.
