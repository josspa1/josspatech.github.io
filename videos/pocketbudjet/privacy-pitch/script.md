# PocketBudJet: 45-Second Privacy Pitch

**Duration:** 45 seconds
**Platform:** YouTube / App Store Preview / Social (Facebook, Twitter)
**Tone:** Educational, reassuring, confident

---

## Shot List

### Shot 1 (0:00 - 0:05)
**Visual:** Dark screen. Red warning icon appears, then Plaid logo with a red X over it.
**Audio/VO:** "What if I told you that you don't have to give every budgeting app your bank login?"
**Text overlay:** "No Bank Login. No Plaid. No Risk."

---

### Shot 2 (0:05 - 0:12)
**Visual:** Split screen — left side shows competitor app asking for bank credentials (scary red text); right side shows PocketBudJet with green checkmarks and a padlock icon.
**Audio/VO:** "YNAB, Monarch, Copilot—they all demand your credentials. PocketBudJet? We never ask. Your password stays yours."
**Text overlay:** "They Want Your Login" | "We Respect Your Privacy"

---

### Shot 3 (0:12 - 0:22)
**Visual:** Phone screen — five import method icons appear in sequence with animations: CSV file, PDF statement, camera (receipt), WiFi scanner, keyboard (manual entry).
**Audio/VO:** "You still get all your transactions in. Download a CSV from your bank. Scan receipts. Upload statements. Use our WiFi scanner. Or type them in manually. Your choice."
**Text overlay:** "Five Ways to Import:" → "CSV • PDF • Camera • WiFi Scanner • Manual"

---

### Shot 4 (0:22 - 0:32)
**Visual:** Phone screen — Dashboard with AES-256 lock animation, then show WiFi sync icon between two phones over WiFi, then a "your choice" badge for optional cloud sync.
**Audio/VO:** "No name, no account numbers, no personal info. You pick where your data lives — local by default, or a cloud provider you choose. Backups are AES-256 encrypted with a key only you know."
**Text overlay:** "No Personal Info" → "You Pick Where It Lives" → "AES-256 Encrypted Backups"

---

### Shot 5 (0:32 - 0:45)
**Visual:** Phone screen — Export menu showing 6 formats (PDF, Excel, Word, OFX, HTML, Accountant), price card showing $9.99/month with privacy badge, Josspa mascot gives thumbs up.
**Audio/VO:** "Export anytime in six formats. Take your data anywhere. Privacy-first budgeting shouldn't cost more. At nine ninety-nine a month, Pocket Budget is the most transparent — and still the cheapest."
**Text overlay:** "Export to 6 Formats" → "Only $9.99/month" → "Privacy First."

---

## Production Notes

- **Visual Hierarchy:** Make the contrast between competitor (scary) and PocketBudJet (safe) very clear.
- **Icons:** Use standard lock, WiFi, cloud-blocked, CSV/PDF icons for immediate recognition.
- **Tone:** Reassuring but not preachy. Address privacy concerns directly but avoid FUD (fear, uncertainty, doubt) about competitors.
- **Text Overlays:** Bold, minimal text. White or gold on dark blue.
- **Audio:** Calm, confident voice. Background music slightly slower than the overview video.
- **Target Audience:** Privacy-conscious users, people who distrust tech companies, anyone burned by data breaches.
