## Production Notes

- **Data Presentation:** Use clean, minimal charts. Avoid clutter. One stat per shot.
- **Color Coding:** Green for PocketBudJet advantages, red/grey for competitor gaps. Make the visual hierarchy obvious.
- **Font:** Bold, sans-serif. White on dark blue, high contrast for mobile.
- **Competitor Logos:** Use actual logos (ensure proper licensing/usage rights). Keep them small, not the focus.
- **Tone:** Confident but not arrogant. Factual, not emotional. Let the numbers speak.
- **Music:** Upbeat, modern. Slightly more energetic than privacy-pitch video.
- **Pacing:** Quick cuts (2–3 sec per comparison). Keep momentum high.
- **Josspa:** Optional. If used, a confident nod or pointing gesture works well here.
