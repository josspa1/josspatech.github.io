## Slide 0 — Settings → Security

To set up an app lock, go to Settings and tap Security. You'll see options for App Lock, Recovery Key, and your current lock status. If you haven't set up a lock yet, the app is open to anyone who picks up your phone. Setting a lock takes about thirty seconds.

---

## Slide 1 — Choosing Your Lock Type

Tap App Lock and choose how you want to unlock the app — Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode. Face ID and Touch ID use your device's biometric system directly, so Pocket Budget never sees or stores your fingerprint or face data. Passcode is a separate PIN you set inside the app. Pick whichever feels right for you.

---

## Slide 2 — Your Recovery Key

Once you enable a lock, Pocket Budget generates a recovery key. It looks like this: P-B-J, dash, followed by eight groups of four letters and numbers. This key is the only way to reset your lock if you ever get locked out. It's shown once — copy it to your password manager, write it down, or print it. JosspaTech does not have a copy. There is no "forgot my lock" button that calls our server, because our server doesn't know your key exists.

---

## Slide 3 — Managing Your Key

You can view your recovery key status, generate a new one, or remove it entirely in Settings, Security, Recovery Key. If you rotate your key, the old one stops working immediately — save the new one before you dismiss that screen. If you remove the key entirely, a locked-out situation would require a full reinstall and restore from backup, so we recommend keeping one active.

---

## Slide 4 — If You Get Locked Out

Forgot your passcode? Go to Settings, Security, Recovery Key, and choose the Reset option. Enter your recovery key — dashes and spaces are optional, it's not case-sensitive. If the key matches, your lock is cleared immediately. Your transactions, budgets, and history are completely untouched. Then set up a new lock when you're ready.

---

## Slide 5 — Fully Self-Contained

Every part of this works without a network connection. The key is generated on your device, stored only as a secure hash, and validated locally. No account needed, no server contact, no support ticket. Your lock is your business — Pocket Budget just gives you the tools to manage it yourself.
