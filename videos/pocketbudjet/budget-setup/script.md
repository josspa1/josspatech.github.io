## Production Notes

- **Pacing:** Slower and more methodical than the overview. Each step is 10–15 seconds so viewers can follow along.
- **Screen Interaction:** Show actual taps, swipes, and input. Make the UI feel responsive and smooth.
- **Narration:** Encouraging and patient. This is for beginners—explain what's happening and why.
- **Transitions:** Subtle fade or slide between steps. Keep visual consistency with the main brand colors.
- **Callouts:** Highlight key features (auto-categorize, AI Coach) with text overlays or subtle animations.
- **Music:** Gentle, uplifting background track. Not too fast.
- **Accessibility:** Large text, high contrast. Ensure captions are included for viewers with hearing loss.
