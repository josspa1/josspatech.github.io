## Slide 0 — Settings → Data & Backup

Open Pocket Budget and go to Settings, then tap Data and Backup. This is where you control how much history lives on your phone, how long your backup keeps records, and how receipt images are stored. Everything here is a tradeoff you get to make — Pocket Budget gives you the controls and explains the consequences of each choice.

---

## Slide 1 — Backup Retention

This setting controls how far back your backup keeps full transaction details. Options run from six months up to seven years. Older data doesn't disappear — it gets summarized. Annual totals, top spending categories, and net savings are locked in permanently before anything is removed. The summaries are yours forever regardless of which retention window you choose.

---

## Slide 2 — On-Device History

This controls how many months of transactions live directly on your phone for instant access — no backup fetch needed. The minimum is three months, which is the floor Pocket Budget needs to reliably track your budget patterns. You can extend this up to two years if you want more history available offline. The tradeoff is storage space on your device.

---

## Slide 3 — Receipt Image Storage

Receipt images use a three-tier model. Thumbnails — small preview images — always stay on your device so you can always see what the receipt looks like. Full-resolution images live in your backup. And compressed previews, the middle tier, stay on-device for a window you choose here — thirty, sixty, ninety, or one hundred eighty days. After that window, previews are removed locally but can be fetched from backup any time you tap a receipt.

---

## Slide 4 — Annual Summaries

Scroll down to see your Historical Summaries — year-by-year totals that are computed and locked in permanently before any data ages out. Income, expenses, and net savings for every year you've used the app. These summaries power your all-time analysis even if the underlying transactions are no longer on your device. They're never deleted.

---

## Slide 5 — Free Up Space

Tap "Review and Free Up Space" to see exactly what would be removed before anything happens. The preview shows you how many transactions fall outside your retention window, how many receipt images would move to backup-only, and how much space you'd reclaim. Annual summaries are always computed and locked first — nothing leaves your device until the math is done and the backup confirms it has the data.

---

## Slide 6 — Archive History

The Archive History section shows you the status of every year that's been processed — how many transactions were archived, whether your backup has confirmed receipt, and when it completed. If a year shows as pending, it means the backup hasn't confirmed yet. Transactions stay on your device until that confirmation comes in, so your data is never removed before it's safely stored.
