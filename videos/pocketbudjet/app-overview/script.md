## Production Notes

- **Screen Recording:** Use Android or iOS simulator at 1080x2400 (mobile) or 1125x2436 (iPhone).
- **Pacing:** Fast cuts (2–4 sec per scene) keep energy high.
- **Font:** Sans-serif, white or gold on dark blue (#1A4F7A) backgrounds, high contrast for mobile playback.
- **Audio:** Upbeat, modern background track (royalty-free). VO should be warm and conversational, not robotic.
- **Josspa Mascot:** Optional but recommended for branding consistency. Use at key moments (intro, outro, feature highlights).
- **Transition:** Smooth fades or slide transitions between shots. Keep it snappy for mobile viewers.
