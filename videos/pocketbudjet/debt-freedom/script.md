# PocketBudJet: 60-Second "See Your Debt-Free Date"

**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Platform:** YouTube / App Store Preview / Social (TikTok, Instagram Reels)
**Tone:** Emotional, motivational, empowering

---

## Shot List

### Shot 1 (0:00 - 0:06)
**Visual:** Dark, moody background. Single phone in center. Debt amount appears on screen in large red text: "$45,000 in debt." Concerned expression emoji or Josspa character looking worried.
**Audio/VO:** "Debt is stressful. You know the number. But do you know when you'll be free?"
**Text overlay:** "Debt Stress Is Real." or "The Question: When?"

---

### Shot 2 (0:06 - 0:16)

**Audio/VO:** "PocketBudJet shows you every debt. Credit cards. Student loans. Car payments. All in one place. All tracked."
**Text overlay:** "All Your Debts in One View"

### Shot 3 (0:16 - 0:28)

**Text overlay:** "Snowball vs Avalanche" → "Psychology vs Math"

## Production Notes

- **Emotional Arc:** Start with the pain (stress of debt), escalate with the unknown, climax with the revelation (your freedom date), and resolve with empowerment.
- **Color Shifts:** Red debt cards → gold freedom date. Use color psychology to reinforce emotional journey.
- **Animation:** Confetti, celebratory graphics when the debt-free date is revealed. Make it feel like a real milestone.
- **Voice:** Warm, understanding, then triumphant. This is for people carrying debt—validate their stress, then inspire them.
- **Music:** Starts minor/somber, shifts to uplifting/hopeful as the freedom date appears.
- **Josspa:** Optional but powerful here. Show concern, then celebration. Mascot empathy builds connection.
