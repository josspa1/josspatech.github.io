Step 1 of 8

Spend with intention — not restriction

Pocket Budget's Mindful Features are not about restricting your spending. They're about putting a moment of awareness between impulse and action. You still decide — you just decide with full information.

Step 2 of 8

The spending pause — a moment of intention

When you log a purchase above a threshold you set, Mindful Mode can prompt a brief pause. A few seconds to confirm this fits your plan. No guilt, no judgment — just a moment to make the choice consciously.

Step 3 of 8

Impulse check before you commit

Tap Impulse Check before a purchase you're not sure about. Pocket Budget shows exactly how it affects your remaining budget for the month. Then the decision is entirely yours, made with complete information.

Step 4 of 8

Cooling-off period for big purchases

Set a cooling-off period for purchases over a threshold you choose. Log the item you want, and Pocket Budget reminds you to revisit it after 24 or 48 hours. Most impulse purchases don't survive the wait.

Step 5 of 8

Your mindful spending score

Your Mindful Score tracks how often your spending aligns with your stated intentions. It's not a grade — it's a mirror. Awareness of the pattern is the whole point. Seeing the score change is how the habit forms.

Step 6 of 8

On-budget streaks keep you motivated

Mindful Features tracks your streak of consecutive on-budget days. Streaks are motivating. When you lose one, it's a signal worth noticing — not a failure, just information. The streak starts again immediately.

Step 7 of 8

Budget for fun — guilt-free category

Create a Guilt-Free Spending category in your budget. Spend it on whatever you want, without tracking every detail. Intentional splurging is still intentional — and knowing it's covered removes the anxiety entirely.

Step 8 of 8

Progress gets acknowledged

When you hit a savings goal or stay on budget for a full month, Pocket Budget acknowledges it. Small wins compound. Noticing them — even briefly — is what keeps the momentum going month after month.
