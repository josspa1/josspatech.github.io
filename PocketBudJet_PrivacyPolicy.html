Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 20, 2026 Developer: JosspaTech Contact: [email protected]
The short version: PocketBudJet stores all your financial data on your device only. JosspaTech cannot see your data, has no server that receives it, and never will. Your finances are yours alone.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Enter

All financial data you enter into PocketBudJet — including transaction amounts, descriptions, category names, financial source names, debt balances, interest rates, and goals — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of this data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Bank account numbers or routing numbers
  • Credit card numbers or financial account credentials
  • Passwords to any external services
  • Real-time bank or financial institution data
  • Location data
  • Device contacts or contact lists
  • Social Security numbers or government identification
  • Biometric data of any kind

2. How Your Data Is Stored

All data entered into PocketBudJet is stored locally on your device using SQLite with AES-256 encryption. This data is contained within the app's private storage sandbox and is not accessible to JosspaTech or any third party. Your data does not leave your device.

3. Camera Use

Camera access is requested only for receipt scanning. Receipt images are processed locally on your device using on-device OCR technology. Images are not transmitted to any server. You control whether any scanned data is saved.

4. AI Features and Privacy

PocketBudJet offers two tiers of AI features:

Tier 1 — On-Device AI (always on, free): Category prediction, anomaly detection, and spending forecasts run entirely on your device. No data is ever transmitted for these features.

Tier 2 — PBJ Financial Coach (optional, premium, must be explicitly enabled): When you choose to enable the AI Financial Coach, aggregated numerical summaries of your finances are transmitted to Anthropic, Inc. (the maker of the Claude AI) to generate personalized guidance. Specifically, only the following is ever sent:

  • Monthly income total and monthly spending total
  • Spending totals per category (e.g. "Groceries: $380" — never the individual transactions)
  • Total debt balance and number of debts
  • Calculated savings rate percentage

What is NEVER sent to Anthropic or any third party: Individual transaction amounts, merchant names, transaction descriptions, account names, account numbers, dates of specific purchases, or any information that could identify you personally.

The AI Financial Coach is disabled by default and can be turned on or off at any time in Settings. You can use PocketBudJet fully and indefinitely without ever enabling this feature. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of the aggregated data received. Anthropic does not receive your name, email, or any identifying information — only the anonymized financial summaries described above.

5. Bank Account Connectivity (Future Optional Feature)

A future version of PocketBudJet will offer an optional feature to connect your bank accounts for on-demand transaction import. This feature will:

  • Be entirely opt-in — disabled by default and never enabled without your explicit action
  • Use Plaid, Inc. as the bank connectivity provider — JosspaTech will never see your bank credentials
  • Only sync when you manually tap "Sync Now" — nothing connects automatically unless you choose a schedule
  • Show you every transaction for your review before anything is imported
  • Store access tokens only on your device in encrypted secure storage — never transmitted to JosspaTech

This feature is not available in the current version. This policy will be updated with full details before that feature is released. You will be notified in-app before it becomes available.

6. Voice Shortcuts

Voice shortcuts (Siri on iOS, Google Assistant on Android) are an optional opt-in feature that is disabled by default. If you choose to enable voice shortcuts, voice audio is processed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies — not by JosspaTech. No financial data, balances, or transaction history is transmitted. Only the text of your spoken command is used to create a transaction locally on your device.

7. Subscription and Payment Data

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store. JosspaTech does not collect, store, or process payment card information. All billing is handled by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies.

8. Analytics

The current version of PocketBudJet does not collect analytics, usage data, or crash reports. If this changes in a future version, this policy will be updated and users will be notified in advance.

9. Anonymized Merchant Data

To improve auto-categorization accuracy for all PocketBudJet users, the app may periodically send anonymized merchant-to-category mappings to JosspaTech. For example: "Target → Groceries" or "Shell → Transportation." This data is:

  • Sent only over WiFi, in weekly batches
  • Completely anonymous — no user ID, device ID, IP address, or any identifier is included
  • Limited to merchant business names and their assigned category — never transaction amounts, dates, descriptions, or any financial data
  • Public business information, not personal data

This feature is enabled by default. You can turn it off at any time in Settings → Data & Privacy → "Help Improve Categorization." Turning it off immediately deletes any queued data that has not yet been sent. JosspaTech may use the aggregated merchant database for commercial purposes, including licensing to third parties.

10. Children's Privacy

PocketBudJet is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us at [email protected].

11. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored exclusively on your device, you can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. JosspaTech does not retain any copy of your data and has nothing to delete on our end.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the effective date when we do. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes we will provide in-app notification.
