How PocketBudJet Works

Your finances
are your business.

Most finance apps make money from your data. PocketBudJet makes money from a fair price — and that's the whole business model. Your attention is not the product.

Personal data stored on your device

Transactions, budgets, goals, and debt data live in an AES-256 encrypted local database on your phone. JosspaTech has no access to it.

No bank login required

Import as many months of bank statements as you want (PDF, CSV, OFX, QFX) and compare your spending month to month. Scan receipts, capture from email, or enter manually. PocketBudJet never asks for your banking credentials.

No account required — ever

Open the app and start. No email, no password, no profile. When you delete the app, your data goes with it.

AI coach — on your device, on your terms

Ask the AI Financial Coach anything about your money in plain English. It uses only aggregated summaries — category totals, not individual transactions. Opt-in. Opt-out any time in Settings.
